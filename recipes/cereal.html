Breakfast Cereal

Description

You've done it all. Filet Mignon, Boullabaisse, and Beef Wellington. Now you're ready for the big leagues, son, and you're gonna need all the skills you've got. Today we're staring down the barrel of a bowl of cereal. No matter who you are, you'll fail the first few times. Even Julia Childs once wrote "To make cereal one must first find the darkest part of one's soul and be consumed by it". You think you're ready? You're wrong.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cereal (chef's choice of brand)
  • 1 cup of milk (plant-based preferred)
  • 1 bowl
  • 1 spoon

Steps

  1. Shake box of cereal to mix large and small pieces
  2. Carefully pour cereal into bowl
  3. Pour milk into bowl over cereal
  4. Eat cereal QUICKLY! Consider soggy cereal a failure
