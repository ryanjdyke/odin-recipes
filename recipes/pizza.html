Pepperoni Pizza

Description

You ready for some 'za? This is the stuff my nonna used to make. I remember when I was a young boy before the first world war, she would pop a pie in the oven and that's when you knew you had to come home from the coal mines before every slice was gone. Ahh what it was like to be four years old then. I'd go back in a heartbeat. When I make this recipe, I can almost smell the sulfery fumes of the mine again.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 package of pepperoni slices

Steps

  1. Pour flour and water into a bowl and mix
  2. Roll out dough into a circle
  3. Add tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni
  4. Bake at 400F for 15 minutes
